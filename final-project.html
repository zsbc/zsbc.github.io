Final Project

Our final project idea was to make a Checkers Game. There are two different kind
of chips. In our game, we have the red chips and blue chips. To win our game,
the goal is to capture all the opponenets chips. Our game has scoreboards that tell
you who's turn it is, and what score each team has. These scoreboards are clickable as well.


Instructions - How To Play

● 1) First determine what chip color you want
● 2) Check the scoreboard on who's turn it is first
● 3) Click on your color chip and move it up (if its blue), down (if its red)
(If you're a king, regardless of color, the chip can move up or down)
● 4) You can only move 1 spot up or down diagonally (only on black tiles)
● 5) To win, you have to capture all of the opponenets chips, goodluck!
(There is only 1 way to capture which is diagonally (up to 3 chips)
