My Javascripts

Here you can view all my Javascripts applications. All of these
applications were written in my CSC 106 class at the University of Rhode
Island. Khan Academy was used as a tool to give me knowledge on how to
write these applications.

Dancing Bitmoji

A program that makes my bitmoji "dance".

Refresh the page to see it "dance".


Racing Bitmoji

A program that is designed to make my bitmoji
race a frog.

Refresh the page to see it race.


Bitmoji of Different Sizes

Program redesigned to include functions
and the ability to resize my bitmoji.
Click to draw bitmoji on screen.


Music Genre Program

Program designed to let you know what
music to listen to depending on what
you're doing / your current emotions.
Click to see what music to listen to.


Bitmoji Follow Program

Program designed to make my bitmoji follow
your mouse until it hits "out of bounds"
when going down in the canvas.
Hover over to test it out!


Formatted Integers Program

Program designed to pick a number between 1-300
and display it countinig in rows/columns
from left to right on the canvas.


Bitmoji Tag

Program designed to destroy bitmoji's when clicking.


Contacts App

Program designed to to display contact information
when you click a contact on the canvas.
